AARAVAM

Place:Amrita school of engineering,chennai.

Date:30.08.2023

Time:9 AM

Onam, the vibrant and cultural festival of Kerala, is celebrated with great zeal and enthusiasm across the state. A unique and captivating aspect of Onam is the way it fosters unity, cultural diversity, and a sense of camaraderie among people. "Aaravam," the Onam celebration in our college, encapsulates the spirit of this festival and brings together students from various backgrounds to rejoice in the rich traditions of Kerala. The air is filled with excitement as the campus prepares to welcome "Aaravam." Colorful floral rangolis, or "Pookalam," adorn the entrances, each one a masterpiece crafted by the artistic hands of our students. These intricate designs not only add to the festive ambience but also symbolize the prosperity and unity that Onam stands for.
